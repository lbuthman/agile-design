- By placing users at the center of your development process, you remove ambiguity and get to the heart of what
they need.

- UX describes the entire experience of a software product, it is greater than the sum of its parts.

- Usability is not subjective, it is based on informed data, studies and the removal of assumptions.

- User centered design helps us focus on the user's core-needs, preventing us from forcing the problem to fit the tech.

- A common thing in the study of usability is asking why.
